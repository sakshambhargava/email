360tf in partnership with FCI brings to you the first of its kind ‘360tf Export Finance Survey 2023’ for Indian Corporates. In this first edition the survey focuses on hearing from Indian corporates on how they conduct exports and the type of financing challenges they face. The outcome of the survey will provoke policy and practise changes required to support export finance in India. Initial survey findings will be communicated to Banks, Development Financial Institutions and Fintechs at the FCI (Factoring Chain International) and IFSCA (International Financial Services Centres Authority) regional seminar in GIFT City in February 2023.

About 360tf

360tf is a global trade finance fintech bridging importers and exporters to Banks, NBFCs, Financial Institutions and Development Financial Institutions to provide mutually beneficial terms of trade. 360tf serves as a bridge for international trade from more than 70 countries and has coverage of all major trade states in India.
